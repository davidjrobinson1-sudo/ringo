Have Questions?

Frequently Answered Queries

How do I link my payment card with Ringo?

Our smartphone onboarding application lets you securely link your current Visa or Mastercard debit or credit cards via certified financial partner tokenization systems.

Does the ring require periodic recharging?

No. Ringo uses a fully passive NFC antenna array architecture. It safely harvests energy directly from the reading field of standard contactless payment terminals, meaning it never requires charging.

Is my personal banking data safe if I lose the ring?

Absolutely. Ringo transmits dynamic tokenized codes instead of raw card numbers during execution. If misplaced, you can lock or deactivate your ring instantly inside our companion application.

Can I submerge the ring underwater safely?

Yes. Ringo's Zirconia ceramic casing is entirely solid-state and hermetically sealed. It is waterproof up to 50 meters, meaning you can wear it while swimming, washing hands, or showering without issue.
